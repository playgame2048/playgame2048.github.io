📘 How to Play Wordle

📌 Game Rules

Guess the hidden 5‑letter word in 6 attempts. After each guess, tile colors show how close you are: green = correct letter & spot, yellow = right letter wrong spot, gray = letter not in word.

💡 Tips & Tricks

Start with a word containing common vowels and consonants (e.g., "RAISE"). Use process of elimination. Don’t forget: letters can appear more than once!
