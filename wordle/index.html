📖 How to Play Wordle

📌 Game Rules

Guess the hidden 5‑letter word in six attempts. After each guess, the tiles change color to show how close you are: green = correct letter & position, yellow = correct letter, wrong spot, gray = letter not in word.

🧠 Strategy Tips

Start with a word rich in vowels and common consonants (e.g., "ARISE", "STARE"). Use the feedback to eliminate letters. Remember that letters can appear twice!

🌟 Why Wordle is Popular

Its simplicity, daily challenge, and shared results make it a global phenomenon. It's a perfect blend of logic, vocabulary, and fun – all in under 10 minutes.
