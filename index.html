How to Play 2048

2048 is a sliding tile puzzle game. Your goal is to combine tiles of the same number to reach the 2048 tile.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys on PC or swipe on mobile.
  • Combine tiles with the same number.
  • Every move adds a new tile (2 or 4).

How to Win

  • Keep your biggest tile in one corner.
  • Build the row/column around that corner.
  • Avoid moving the big tile away from the corner.

Tips

  • Plan 2 moves ahead.
  • Don't swipe randomly.
  • Keep tiles organized by size.
