      📘 How to Play Crossword Mini

      Basic Rules

      Fill the 7×7 grid so every row and column forms a word. Numbers show where words start. Use the clues on the right.

      • One letter per cell
      • No skipping
      • All words valid

      Pro Techniques

      Use crossing letters. Start with words you know, then use intersecting letters to guess others.

      • Look for common prefixes
      • Vowels appear often
      • Check word length

      Smart Tips

      Highlighted row/col helps focus. Red cell means double-check. Practice daily to improve.

      • Use process of elimination
      • Keep a steady pace
