How to Play Connect 4

📋 Game Rules

Players take turns dropping discs into a 7x6 grid. The disc falls to the lowest empty cell in the column. First to connect four horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.

🧠 Winning Strategies

Control the center, plan ahead, block opponent’s threats. Look for "traps" where you create two winning options at once.

⚡ Pro Tips

In AI mode, observe random moves – but you can exploit patterns. Use the diagonal lines early. Never play directly under an opponent's disc without blocking.
