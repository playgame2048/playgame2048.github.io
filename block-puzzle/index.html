How to Play

Block Puzzle is a classic block-dropping game inspired by Tetris. Your goal is simple: place the falling blocks in the grid and complete full horizontal rows to clear them.

  • Use Left and Right to move blocks
  • Use Up to rotate blocks
  • Use Down to drop faster
  • Each cleared row gives you points
