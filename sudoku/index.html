How to Play Sudoku

Sudoku is a logic-based puzzle game. Your goal is to fill the grid with numbers 1–9 without repeating in any row, column, or 3×3 block.

How to Win

  • Start with the easiest numbers.
  • Use logic, not guessing.
  • Check rows, columns, and blocks for conflicts.
  • Use highlighting to track your moves.
  • Avoid mistakes — 4 errors = Game Over.
