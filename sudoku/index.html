Sudoku Pro

Simple • Clean • Professional

How to Play

Fill the grid with numbers 1 to 9 without repeating any number in each row, column, or 3×3 block.

Tap a cell, then choose a number from the pad. Use "Erase" to clear a cell.

Tips & Strategy

  • Start with cells that have fewer possible numbers.
  • Use logic, not guessing.
  • Check rows, columns, and blocks often.
