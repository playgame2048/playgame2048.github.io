How to Play

Tic Tac Toe is played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O). The first player to align three symbols in a row, column, or diagonal wins.

How to Win

Try to control the center, block your opponent early, and always look two moves ahead. Against AI, forcing a draw is also a smart strategy.
