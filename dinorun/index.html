🎮 how to play

Press SPACE/ARROW UP (or tap the game) to make the dino jump. Avoid obstacles, they get faster over time. Survive as long as you can!

⚡ tips & tricks

Early jumps give better control. The dino can jump again only after landing. High score is saved locally. Can you beat your record?
