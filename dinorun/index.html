🕹️ HOW TO PLAY

▶ Press SPACE / ARROW UP or click on game to jump.
▶ Avoid obstacles by jumping over them.
▶ Each passed obstacle = +100 pts.
▶ Game speed slowly increases.
▶ Obstacle spacing varies – sometimes close, sometimes far.

⚡ TIPS

• Tap to jump – no need to hold.
• Stay calm, rhythm is key.
• High score saved in your browser.
• Dark mode reduces eye strain.
• First game over opens a surprise link 🤫
